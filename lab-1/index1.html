Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create machines capable of intelligent behavior. It involves the development of algorithms and systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and understanding natural language. AI has the potential to revolutionize various industries, including healthcare, finance, and transportation.
